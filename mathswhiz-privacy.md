
# Privacy Policy for MathsWhiz

**Last updated:** April 2026

MathsWhiz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed for children and families and does not collect unnecessary personal data.

---

## 📊 Information We Collect

We aim to collect **as little data as possible**.

### 1. Personal Information

MathsWhiz does **not require account creation** and does not collect personal details such as:

* Full name
* Address
* Email address

If a child's name is entered in Settings, it is stored **locally on the device only** and is not transmitted.

---

### 2. Usage Data

We may store the following **locally on your device**:

* Quiz scores
* Progress and accuracy by topic
* Session history
* Settings preferences

This data is used only to improve the user experience and is **not shared externally**.

---

### 3. Analytics (if applicable)

If analytics tools are used (e.g., app performance or usage tracking), they may collect:

* Device type
* App interactions (anonymous)

We ensure this data is:

* Anonymous
* Not linked to a specific user
* Not used for advertising

---

## 🔒 How We Use Data

We use data only to:

* Provide app functionality
* Track learning progress
* Improve app performance

We do **not**:

* Sell data
* Share data with third parties for marketing
* Track users across apps or websites

---

## 👶 Children's Privacy

MathsWhiz is designed for children (KS2 level).

We:

* Do not knowingly collect personal data from children
* Do not include behavioural advertising
* Follow principles aligned with child privacy protection laws

---

## 🛡 Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored:

* Locally on the device
* Securely within the app sandbox

No cloud storage or external servers are used unless explicitly stated.

---

## ⚙️ Your Choices

You can:

* Reset progress at any time via Settings
* Delete the app to remove all stored data
